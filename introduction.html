Introduction to
UOWYFGZ

This blog has will create over thousands of words aimed at bringing up undiluted opinions on social issues which will span across issues affecting this generation, spirituality, pop culture, and everything about everything.

Unpopular Opinions With Your Favourite Gen-Z is an upcoming podcast and blog that will be broadcasted on all platforms including Apple Music and Spotify. There will be episodes released once every week and they will be divided into different seasons. The project we have in mind will create over thousands of minutes aimed at bringing up undiluted opinions on social issues which will span across issues affecting this generation, spirituality, pop culture, and basically everything about everything.With having invited guests over on special episodes, the podcast hopes to bridge the gap between one perspective and another. Through in-person and virtual conversations, UOWYGZ hopes to bring in people of great intellect and emotional to speak on sensitive issues.

Proper recording will start towards the end of 2022 and releases of the first three episodes will be done after editing is complete. The podcast for the first season will have around 10-12 episodes (this may vary). UOWYGZ will have social media platforms such as Twitter, Instagram and Tiktok so as to interact with the regular listeners and get a wider range of listeners.
